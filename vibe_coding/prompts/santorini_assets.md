### card composition
```
You are composing a final board game card image using three provided images.

INPUT IMAGES:
1. A vertical card background frame (frame_image).
2. A character illustration of a Greek god (god_illustration).
3. A set of minimalist action icons (action_icons).

TASK:
Create a clean, professional card composition by combining the three images.

COMPOSITION RULES:
- Use the frame_image as the base layer.
- Place the god_illustration in the upper section of the card:
  - Centered horizontally.
  - Occupying approximately the top 65–70% of the card height.
  - Do not stretch or distort the illustration.
  - Respect transparency if present.

- Place the action_icons in the lower section of the card:
  - Centered horizontally.
  - Occupying approximately the bottom 25–30% of the card height.
  - Keep icons grouped and readable.
  - Maintain original proportions and spacing.

LAYOUT CONSTRAINTS:
- Do NOT crop important parts of the illustration or icons.
- Do NOT overlap the illustration and icon areas.
- Do NOT add any text, titles, numbers, or UI elements.
- Do NOT recolor or stylize the images.
- Preserve the original visual style of each asset.

VISUAL STYLE:
- Clean, elegant, board-game-ready composition.
- Balanced spacing and alignment.
- No additional effects, shadows, glows, or decorations.

OUTPUT:
- A single vertical card image.
- High resolution.
- Suitable for direct use in a digital board game.
```
